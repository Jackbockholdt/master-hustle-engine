Cannabinoid & Terpene Breakdown

CannabinoidPsychoactive?Commonly Reported Effects
CBDNoRelaxed, clear-headed, calming. No high.
CBGMinimal at typical dosesMellow focus, mood lift. The "mother cannabinoid" other cannabinoids are made from.
CBNMildlySedating, sleepy — forms as THC ages/degrades.
Delta-9 THCYesThe classic "high" — euphoria, body relaxation.
THCVYes, at higher dosesClear-headed, energetic, often described as uplifting/giggly rather than heavy.
THCPYes — strongBinds much harder to CB1 in lab studies than Delta-9; reports describe a more intense, longer-lasting high. Human research is still thin, so go slow.
The "laughing feeling like mushrooms" question, straight up: mushrooms work through a completely different pathway (serotonin receptors) than anything in cannabis (which works through CB1/CB2 receptors) — so no cannabinoid is chemically the same thing. What people call the giggly, laughing high in weed is usually a terpene + cannabinoid combo: sativa-leaning strains high in limonene and pinene, paired with THCV, tend to get reported as more energetic and giggly instead of a heavy couch-lock body high. Durban Poison (already in the Strain Vault above) is a classic example people describe that way.
