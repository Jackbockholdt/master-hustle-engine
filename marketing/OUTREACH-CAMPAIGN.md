## PART 1 — LinkedIn DM Sequence (3 steps)

Short, native, no links in the first message (LinkedIn suppresses reach on link-first DMs). Goal: a reply, then a 15-minute screen share.

### DM 1 — Connect / Opener
> Hey [First Name] — I work with agency owners who want to add an AI automation line without building or hiring for it. You white-label a production engine, put your brand on it, and resell it to your own clients on a monthly. Open to me sending over the 2-minute version?

You license the engine, resell access to your clients, and the math carries the pitch — resell to 3 clients at $500/mo and you're already break-even on the monthly license, everything after is margin. Setup is $2,500, then $1,500/mo. If you'd rather own it outright there's a one-time buyout too. Worth a 15-min screen share so you can see the actual dashboard, not a deck?

## Sequence Rules

| Step | Channel | Timing | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| DM 1 | LinkedIn | Day 0 | Get a reply / permission |
| DM 2 | LinkedIn | On reply or day 2–3 | Deliver the math |
| DM 3 | LinkedIn | Day 4–5 | Soft close to calendar |
| Email 1 | Email | Day 0 | Intro + math + CTA |
| Email 2 | Email | Day 3 | Retention angle, stronger CTA |
| Email 3 | Email | Day 7 | Final touch, permission to walk away |

- **On any reply (even a no):** halt the sequence and respond personally. Reading replies is a human job.
- **On a booked call:** halt the sequence and start onboarding.
- **On a purchase:** deliver access manually via GitHub repo invite, then run the onboarding call.
- **After email 3 with no reply:** move to a 90-day re-engagement list; don't keep active outreach.
- **Opt-outs:** anyone who says stop/unsubscribe goes on the do-not-contact list (see `CLAUDE.md`).
