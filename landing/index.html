Questions

Do I need a developer to use this?

No. The engine is built, hosted, and maintained. You license it, brand it, and resell it — you never touch a line of code. (The buyout option is for operators who do want to run it themselves.)

How do my clients pay — and how do I make money?

You set your own resale price and bill your clients directly. Resell to three clients at $500/month and you've covered the $1,500 monthly license; everything beyond that is margin.

What's the difference between the license and the buyout?

The license ($2,500 setup + $1,500/mo) keeps it hosted and maintained while you resell under your brand. The buyout ($25,000 one-time) hands you the codebase outright to own and operate yourself, with no recurring fee.

How do I get access after I buy?

Access is delivered personally — a GitHub repo invite and an onboarding call — so you're set up right rather than dropped into a portal.
